aboutus

we take care of your healthy life

Endo Life Super Speciality Hospitals, is reputed tertiary center in AP, where treatments for all endocrinology related problems, viz., uncontrolled diabetes, Thyroid, Obesity, Hormones, endocrine surgeries, radiation therapies & nuclear scans services are available under one roof.

Started in 2006, by an Endocrinologist, Dr. Srikanth Kongara, a gold medallist from GGH, MD from prestigious AIIMS, New Delhi and DM (Endocrinology) from SGPGI, Lucknow. Endo Life currently operates from a fully air conditioned and hygiene premises, situated at Kothapet, Guntur, which is 1-2 KMs radius from Bus Stand and Railway station.
